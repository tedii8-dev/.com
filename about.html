My Ethiopian Tech Journey

"Selam World!"

My story begins in Addis Ababa, where as a child I would watch the city's bustling tech hubs with fascination. While other kids played football, I sketched website layouts in my notebook, dreaming of one day building digital bridges for Ethiopia. The African sun would set as I practiced HTML tags by lamplight during power outages - my first lessons in resilience.

who am I?

During high school in Ginbot 20, I turned our family computer into a coding lab. Between bunna breaks, I built my first websites - clunky but full of passion. I learned that Ethiopian ingenuity could overcome slow internet and limited resources. Each project carried bits of our culture into the digital world.

Werkama Zemen - The Golden Era

Today, I stand at the intersection of Ethiopian tradition and modern web development. From the ancient wisdom of Axum to the innovative spirit of Sheba Valley, my work carries forward our legacy. I build not just websites, but digital mesob - spaces where ideas gather and flourish. This is my desta, my joy - bringing Ethiopian excellence to the global digital landscape.
